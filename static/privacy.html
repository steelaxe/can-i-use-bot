Bot Privacy Policy

Personal Information Management

We will keep personal information accurate and up-to-date, and in order to prevent unauthorized access/loss/corruption/
falsification/leakage, We will maintain our security-system and manage-system and do safety-measures so that we can control personal information strictly

Why we have to use personal information

We use personal information for improve our bot. We have been analyzing user actions by Mixpanel. If you mind tracking, please click here to opt out.
And, if you contact us, we use personal information for messaging or answering your questions or sending e-mails or documents.

Prohibition from disclosing personal information to others

We will manage customer's personal information properly, and We won't disclose personal information to others except below cases

  • If you have your agreement
  • If you want to use some services provided by other companies which requires us to use your information.
  • If the disclosure is necessary based on a law

Privacy Safety Measures

We use a variety of security measures to make sure we protect your personal information.

Personal Inquiry

If a customer wants to inquiry/modify/delete your personal information, We'll deal with it after confirming the user himself/herself.

Compliance and Revision

We will abide by Japanese law and any other rules regarding personal information we retain. and We sill improve this policypolicy accordingly
